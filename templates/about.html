Project Overview

About TB Detection AI

TB Detection AI is an artificial intelligence–based clinical decision support system designed to assist in the early detection of tuberculosis using chest X-ray images. The system analyzes radiographic images using deep learning techniques to identify patterns associated with pulmonary tuberculosis. It helps healthcare professionals quickly screen patients and identify potential TB cases for further medical evaluation.

Problem Statement

The Need for AI in TB Screening

Tuberculosis remains one of the leading infectious diseases worldwide. Early detection is crucial for effective treatment and preventing transmission. However, manual interpretation of chest X-rays requires experienced radiologists and can be time-consuming. AI-based systems can assist in faster screening and support medical professionals in detecting TB cases efficiently.

Process

System Workflow

1
User uploads a chest X-ray image

Secure DICOM or PNG/JPG image upload via the clinical dashboard.

2
Image preprocessing and normalization

Grayscale conversion, resizing to 224x224, and tensor normalization.

3
Deep learning model analyzes the X-ray

The ResNet-50 feature extractor identifies radiological patterns.

4
System predicts Normal or Tuberculosis

Classification into one of the two diagnostic categories.

5
Confidence score and risk level are calculated

Probability analysis determines Low, Moderate, or High risk levels.

6
Grad-CAM heatmap highlights influential regions

Explainable AI highlights regions with high-activation features.

Interpretability

Explainable AI (Grad-CAM)

The system uses Grad-CAM (Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping) to generate visual explanations of the AI model's predictions. It highlights regions in the chest X-ray that influenced the model's decision, improving transparency and interpretability for clinicians.
